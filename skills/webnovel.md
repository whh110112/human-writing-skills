# Web Novel Skill

## Aim

Write serialized genre fiction with momentum, continuity, and memorable scene turns.

## Continuity Priorities

- Track power levels, abilities, resources, injuries, secrets, promises, and relationship shifts.
- Do not solve a conflict by inventing an unseeded ability.
- Keep chapter endings linked to active tension.
- Reuse prior details as payoff.
- Let each scene change status, knowledge, danger, intimacy, or leverage.

## Style Controls

- Use clean, fast prose during action.
- Give important reveals a short beat before reaction.
- Keep names, titles, and factions consistent.
- Balance inner thought with visible pressure.
- End with a hook that follows from the scene, not a random shock.

## Avoid

- forgetting injuries or costs
- repeated awe reactions
- explaining the whole system in one block
- resetting relationships after major scenes
