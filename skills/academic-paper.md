# Academic Paper Skill

## Aim

Produce formal, cautious, well-scaffolded research prose.

## Structure

- Define the research object and scope.
- Explain the gap or problem before the contribution.
- Distinguish observation, method, result, and interpretation.
- Use citations or citation placeholders when evidence is required.
- Make limitations explicit.

## Style Controls

- Prefer restrained claims.
- Keep terminology consistent.
- Avoid decorative language.
- Use topic sentences that identify the paragraph's function.
- Preserve methodological detail when summarizing.

## Avoid

- unsupported novelty claims
- vague "significant impact" language
- mixing literature review, method, and conclusion in one paragraph
- claiming causality from descriptive evidence
