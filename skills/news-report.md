# News Report Skill

## Aim

Write clear, source-aware news copy with restrained language and strong factual order.

## Structure

- Lead with the newest, most important verified fact.
- Put time, place, actor, action, and impact early.
- Attribute claims, estimates, accusations, and opinions.
- Separate confirmed facts from background and analysis.
- Keep paragraphs short.

## Style Controls

- Use neutral verbs unless evidence supports stronger wording.
- Prefer exact numbers and dates over vague scale.
- Avoid adjectives that imply judgment.
- Preserve chronology when it matters.

## Avoid

- promotional phrasing
- unsupported causal claims
- anonymous "some people say" framing
- burying the main fact below background
