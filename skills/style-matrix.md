# Style Matrix

## Aim

Prevent "one human voice" from being applied to every genre. Each style has its own permissions, risks, and signs of life.

| Style | Human Signal | Useful Modules | Risk |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Fiction | pressure, action, subtext, sensory specificity | embodied-emotion, vocal-rhythm, controlled-drift, natural-measurement, spatial-blocking, occupancy-capacity, cliche-phrase-audit, prose-progress-audit | overexplaining feelings or losing blocking |
| Essay | stance, doubt, concrete examples, fair concessions | controlled-drift, imperfect-prose, ai-trace-rubric, formulaic-structure-audit, prose-progress-audit | generic balance and safe conclusion |
| News report | verified order, attribution, compression | ai-trace-rubric, prose-progress-audit | too much personality |
| Self-media | judgment, anecdote, usefulness, spoken rhythm | imperfect-prose, cultural-anchors, vocal-rhythm, cliche-phrase-audit, formulaic-structure-audit | clickbait and fake intimacy |
| Academic paper | cautious claim, method clarity, term stability | ai-trace-rubric, formulaic-structure-audit, prose-progress-audit | decorative prose or unsupported novelty |
| Web novel | continuity, payoff, cliffhanger, status change | embodied-emotion, vocal-rhythm, cultural-anchors, relationship-state, occupancy-capacity, physical-continuity-audit, cliche-phrase-audit, prose-progress-audit | invented convenience, relationship reset, forgotten costs, capacity/prop drift |
| Memoir/Essay | memory, contradiction, voice, reflective gaps | controlled-drift, imperfect-prose, embodied-emotion | sentimentality |
| Commercial copy | pain point, proof, case detail, low-friction offer | cultural-anchors, vocal-rhythm, ai-trace-rubric, cliche-phrase-audit | hard-sell adjectives |

## Selection Rule

Start with one primary style. Add at most three modules unless the task asks for a deep editorial pass.
