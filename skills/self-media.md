# Self-Media Skill

## Aim

Write posts that feel direct, useful, and personal without empty hype.

## Style Controls

- Start from a concrete observation, conflict, mistake, or useful result.
- Keep the speaker's stance clear.
- Use short sections, but avoid clickbait rhythm.
- Include lived details, numbers, or examples when available.
- Let personality come from judgment and phrasing, not slogans.

## Avoid

- "must read" pressure
- stacked rhetorical questions
- fake intimacy
- over-polished motivational endings
- repeating the same sentence shape across bullets

## Revision Check

Remove any sentence that could appear unchanged in a post about a completely different topic.
