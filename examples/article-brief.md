# Article Brief Example

## Topic

Why long-form AI writing loses continuity and how lightweight ledgers can reduce drift.

## Audience

Writers, editors, product builders, and prompt engineers.

## Position

Long context windows help, but they do not replace explicit state tracking. A ledger gives the model a compact memory of commitments it must honor.

## Evidence To Mention

- names and facts drift first when text grows
- summaries are useful only when they preserve unresolved promises
- style instructions work better when paired with examples and revision checks

## Tone

Practical, clear, not academic-heavy.
